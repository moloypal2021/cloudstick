What happens to my server after I cancel a subscription ?

After cancelling your subscription, all data remains on your servers: we do not remove any data. When you renew your account with CloudStick, you are able to manage all of your servers again if you didn’t reinstall them

Will I get Team Features along with all plans ?

Team features are included in the Basic and Pro Plans

Yes, we will begin charging you monthly/yearly based on the subscription type you have chosen
